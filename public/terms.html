Work From Home Employee Terms & Conditions

Company Name: PrimeGrow Services

Position: Telecaller / Sales Executive (Work From Home)

Work Mode: Remote (Work From Home)

1. Job Responsibility

Employee का मुख्य कार्य कंपनी की Digital Marketing Services (GMB Optimization, Lead Generation, Website, Social Media Marketing आदि) को potential clients को promote करना और sales generate करना होगा।

Employee को निम्न कार्य करने होंगे:

  • Daily calling / follow-ups करना
  • Interested clients को company services explain करना
  • Sales close करना
  • Daily work report submit करना
  • Company guidelines के अनुसार professional behaviour maintain करना

2. Working Hours

Employee को प्रतिदिन minimum 6-8 घंटे काम करना होगा।

Daily tasks में शामिल होगा:

  • Calling
  • Lead follow up
  • Client communication
  • Reporting

3. Salary Structure

Basic Salary: ₹10,000 per month

Salary structure performance based होगा।

Target Requirement

Employee को हर महीने minimum 10 sales complete करनी होंगी।

Commission Structure

Sales Commission
First 10 Sales Included in Basic Salary
11th Sale onward ₹200 per Sale

Example:

10 sales = ₹10,000 salary

15 sales = ₹10,000 + ₹1000 commission

20 sales = ₹10,000 + ₹2000 commission

4. Payment Terms

Salary monthly basis पर release की जाएगी।

Payment तभी release होगा जब:

  • Target complete हो
  • Proper reporting हो
  • Client payment received हो

5. Work Discipline

  • Fake promises नहीं करने
  • Client से respectful communication रखना
  • Company data leak नहीं करना
  • Company policies follow करना

6. Termination Policy

Company को अधिकार है कि:

  • Poor performance
  • Misbehaviour
  • Data misuse
  • Company rules violation

की स्थिति में employee को immediate terminate किया जा सकता है।

Employee भी 7 days prior notice देकर job छोड़ सकता है।

7. Confidentiality

Employee को company की confidential information जैसे:

  • Client data
  • Leads
  • Sales process
  • Internal strategy

को किसी third party के साथ share नहीं करना होगा।
